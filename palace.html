Ambavilasa (Durbar Hall)

The magnificent hall where the king hosted major public gatherings. Known for its painted steel pillars, stained glass ceiling, and intricate chandeliers.

📍 Location: First Floor, Main Palace Building

Kalyana Mantapa (Marriage Pavilion)

A grand octagonal pavilion with a spectacular stained-glass ceiling featuring peacock motifs, historically used for royal weddings.

📍 Location: South Wing, Palace Complex
